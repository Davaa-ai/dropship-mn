# Agency Positioning & Strategy Context
*Project: Dropship MN / Conversio Agency*
*Target Market: Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia*

## 1. The Core Identity: How You Position Yourself
**Do NOT position yourself as a "Web Developer."** 
Web developers are seen as commodities. People think "I can just build a website on Wix or Shopify." 

**Position yourself as a "Conversion Engineer" or "Sales Funnel Partner."**
You do not build "websites." You build **automated sales machines** designed to liquidate inventory and convert Facebook traffic into actual orders without manual chat support. 

* **The Old Way (Your Competition):** A Facebook shop owner manually replying to messages, losing sales while they sleep, and asking for people to transfer money via chat. It's high friction.
* **The New Way (You):** You provide a Taobao-style, frictionless buying experience where the customer lands, sees a premium product presentation, clicks buy, inputs their info, and the data instantly hits a Telegram bot. 

## 2. Who is the Target Client?
You are looking for people with the highest amount of "pain."
*   **Primary Target:** Importers and Wholesalers (Бөөний төв, Нарантуул, Зайсан importers). They bring in containers of goods (clothes, electronics, home goods) and often get stuck with "Dead Stock" because Facebook posts aren't moving the volume they need.
*   **Secondary Target:** High-ticket service providers (e.g., real estate, specialized coaching) who need a premium landing page to capture leads.
*   **Why Wholesalers?** They have millions in frozen capital. A ₮350,000 - ₮500,000 investment to revive sales is a no-brainer for them. 

## 3. The Core Offers (Pricing Strategy)
Have three clear models ready when speaking to a client:

### A. The "Dead Stock Rescue" (The Setup Model)
*   **Best for:** Importers desperate to move a specific container of slow-moving goods.
*   **The Offer:** You build a high-converting, single-product landing page (like the Baby Chair) and hook it up to their Telegram. 
*   **The Price:** ₮350,000 to ₮750,000 upfront. (This is your primary goal to reach ₮350k/day). 

### B. The Performance Model (The "Baby Chair" Case Study)
*   **Best for:** Products you *know* will sell well, and the owner is risk-averse. 
*   **The Offer:** Zero setup fee. You take a fixed commission (e.g., ₮29,000) per confirmed sale or a high percentage of the markup. Because you control the lead capture (Telegram), you control the data.
*   **Warning:** Only do this if you have high confidence in the product and ad budget to push traffic.

### C. The Hybrid Model (The Sweet Spot)
*   **Best for:** Establishing long-term, scalable relationships. 
*   **The Offer:** A low upfront fee (e.g., ₮200,000) to build the asset + a small commission (e.g., ₮5,000 - ₮10,000) per transaction flowing through your page.

## 5. Next Steps & Execution Logic
1.  **Keep the codebase modular:** In your `dropship-mn` folder, treat `products/baby-chair/` as a template. For a new client, duplicate it to `products/new-product/` and swap out images/CSS variables. This allows you to build a ₮500k page in under 3 hours.
2.  **Gather Social Proof:** The second you get an order on the baby chair page, screenshot the Telegram notification (blur names). Use that screenshot when pitching new clients: *"This is how the orders will hit your phone."*
3.  **Outbound Volume:** To make ₮350k a day, you need to send 10-20 targeted messages a day to business owners.
